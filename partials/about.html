Our Story

How it all started

InterActiveMove was born from a simple observation: children sit too much, while they actually want to move. In 2019, we started with a mission to use technology that gets children away from their screens and into motion.

What started as an idea in a small workshop in Nijkerk, grew into a company that now supplies schools, healthcare facilities, and entertainment centers throughout the Netherlands and beyond with interactive projection systems.

Our strength? We combine proven technology with a deep understanding of how people learn and move. Every product is developed in close collaboration with teachers, therapists, and children themselves.

"We saw how technology kept children glued to screens. We wanted to show it can be different - technology that invites movement."
Founders, InterActiveMove

Who we are

InterActiveMove (IAM) develops interactive projection systems that stimulate children and adults to move and grow cognitively. Our innovative technology is deployed in education, sports, and rehabilitation care.

We believe that movement, learning, and play go hand in hand. By combining advanced projection technology with motion sensors, we create unique experiences that inspire people of all ages.

Our mission & vision

Mission: Inspiring movement through interaction

We want to inspire people to move and learn in an active and playful way. By integrating interactive technologies, we create new possibilities for education, rehabilitation, sports, and entertainment.

  • Stimulating physical activity across all age groups
  • Learning through play - education becomes an adventure
  • Supporting rehabilitation with motivating exercises
  • Socially inclusive: moving and learning together

Vision: Technology and movement seamlessly connected

IAM envisions a world where movement and technology come together seamlessly. We develop solutions that are not only physically attractive but also mentally stimulating and socially interactive.

  • Interactive technology in every school and healthcare facility
  • Movement as a natural part of the day
  • Innovative solutions that make a global impact
  • Continuously developing and improving our products
