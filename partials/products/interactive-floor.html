Frequently asked questions

What exactly is the interactive floor?
The interactive floor is a projection system that projects images onto the ground that respond to movement. With infrared sensors, we detect every step, jump, or touch, adjusting the game image in real-time.
How large is the projection area?
The standard projection area is approximately 3x2.5 meters. This provides enough space for 4-6 children to play simultaneously. Larger surfaces are possible with multiple systems.
How do I keep the floor clean?
The interactive floor itself has no physical surface - it's a projection on your existing floor. You can clean your floor as you normally would. The projector and sensors can be wiped with a dry cloth.
Is installation difficult?
No! The system is completely plug-and-play. Within 10-15 minutes everything is connected and ready to play. With the Professional and Enterprise packages, we handle the complete installation.
For what ages is it suitable?
The interactive floor is designed for children from 3 to 12 years old, but adults enjoy it too! Games are available for specific age groups and skill levels.
What is the minimum ceiling height?
For optimal projection quality, we recommend a minimum ceiling height of 2.7 meters. For lower ceilings, we can work together to find a suitable solution.
