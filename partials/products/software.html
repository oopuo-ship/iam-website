Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need programming knowledge to use the Game Editor?
No, absolutely not! Our Game Editor is specifically designed for non-technical users. With an intuitive drag-and-drop interface, you can get started within minutes. We also offer video tutorials and personal onboarding training.
How long does it take to develop a custom game?
Development time varies per project. A simple branded game can be ready within 2-3 weeks, while a fully custom application with API integrations can take 6-8 weeks. After the intake, we provide you with a precise schedule.
Can I modify the content myself later?
Yes! All games are delivered with a content management system that allows you to modify texts, images and settings yourself. For more complex adjustments, you can contact us or purchase a Game Editor license.
Do I own the IP rights to custom work?
With Full Custom packages, you retain full exclusive rights to all custom-developed content and code. With Branded Games packages, you get exclusive usage rights within your sector. The underlying IAM technology remains property of InterActiveMove.
What support do I receive after delivery?
All packages include at least 3-6 months of free support for bug fixes and technical questions. Full Custom packages receive lifetime priority support. Additionally, we offer optional annual maintenance contracts.
Does the software work on all IAM systems?
Yes, all software we develop is compatible with all our hardware systems: Interactive Floor, Wall, 2-in-1, Sandbox. We ensure your games work optimally on every platform.
