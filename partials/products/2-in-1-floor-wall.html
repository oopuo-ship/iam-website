Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 2-in-1 system exactly?
The 2-in-1 system combines an interactive floor and an interactive wall in one device. With one projector and sensor system, you can project images on the ground or the wall that respond to movement and touch.
Can I project on the floor and wall simultaneously?
The system operates in one mode at a time — floor or wall. Switching between modes is simple and quick. For simultaneous floor and wall projection, you can combine two systems.
How many programs are available?
IAM mobile solo offers 60+ programs for the chosen floor or wall setup. IAM mobile duo and premium offer floor and wall programs (one mode at a time) with 120+ interactive programs in total. The software is included with no subscription or license costs.
Is installation difficult?
Not at all! The system is fully plug-and-play. Everything is connected and ready to play within 15 minutes. With IAM mobile duo and premium, we take care of the complete installation.
What is the advantage over separate systems?
The 2-in-1 system saves costs, space and installation effort. You get two interactive experiences for a lower investment than two separate systems. Ideal for organisations that want maximum flexibility.
What age groups is it suitable for?
The 2-in-1 system is suitable for all ages, from toddlers to seniors. There are programs for specific age groups and skill levels, from educational programs for children to rehabilitation exercises for older adults.
