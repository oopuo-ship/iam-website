Frequently asked questions

How does this fit into our existing lesson plan?
Our games are specifically developed to align with primary education learning objectives. We provide an implementation guide and support integrating the systems into your existing teaching methods.
What training do our teachers receive?
Each package includes comprehensive on-site training. Teachers learn to operate the system, select games, and adapt them to their learning objectives. We also offer online guides and telephone support.
What about maintenance and updates?
All systems come with a 2-year warranty. Software updates are free and automatic. Our service team is available for any technical questions or maintenance needs.
Can we create our own games?
With our Game Editor, teachers can create their own content. Make quizzes matching your curriculum, add your own images, or customize existing games. No technical knowledge required!
Are subsidies available?
Various subsidies are available for educational institutions, including national education programs and regional incentives for physical education. We're happy to help identify opportunities and support your application.
What are the minimum space requirements?
For an interactive floor, we recommend at least 4x3 meters of free floor space with a ceiling height of 2.5 meters. For smaller spaces, we also have compact solutions. We're happy to visit for a free space assessment.
